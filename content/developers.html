Code with us

Find opportunities to contribute to government projects.

Code with Us is our experiment to find a faster, easier, and more effective way for Government to get and pay for code and other artifacts related to a digital service. Using micro-purchases and open source licenses, we’re paying a fixed price for code and artifacts that meet agreed upon acceptance criteria.


What's New

We’ve just introduced a couple of changes based on the feedback we’ve had from participants:

  1. Opportunities up to $5,000 in value. (We’ll be working to raise that amount higher in the coming months, too.)
  2. A formal assignment process.
